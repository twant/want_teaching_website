Academic Policies and Procedures

  • All assignments are due at 11:59 pm on the date listed.

  • Extension requests must be submitted by 8:00 pm the day before the posted due date. Students will be notified of the status of the request before 9:00pm the night before the posted due dates.

  • Assignments submitted late will be assessed 10% per day late. No late assignments will be accepted after 5 days.

  • Every person's coding style is different. Plagerism includes copying another person's code or ideas--if you claim another person's work or thoughts as your own, it will be considered plagerism. Regardless of the weight of the assignment, plagiarism is a serious offense with serious consequences. If you plagiarize an assignment, you will receive NO CREDIT for the assignment and will be referred to your assistant principal with 8 demerits.
